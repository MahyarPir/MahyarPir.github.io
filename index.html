Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2025

Tickity Tockity ("the App") respects your privacy. Here is our policy in plain English:

1. No Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data. We do not have servers, and we do not use any third-party analytics trackers.

2. Your Data Stays on Your Device

All task history, settings, and streak data are stored locally on your device (using Apple's UserDefaults and CoreData) and/or in your private iCloud account (if you have iCloud enabled). The developer cannot access this data.

3. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email above.
